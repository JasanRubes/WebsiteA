However when I arrived at highschool I gave myself a goal to be as involved as possible. Outside of sports I have other hobbies, some were more of a faze but some have become part of my everyday life. Things like archery and airsoft are still fun to do every once and awhile. I play video games much too often but it is a way I can connect with friends who live far away. I have strengths and weaknesses just like anyone else. I am a leader who is not scared to take control and lead a group. I have shown this with my involvement in link crew and student leadership. However, I have had a history of becoming a little bossy when a group of my peers are not functioning like a well oiled machine. I do have good work habits and my parents are not afraid to push me to get my work done. My name is Jasan Rubes and I will try to be the best student possible this year so I not only learn but can exceed expectations and go above and beyond.
